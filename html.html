I’ll tell you a little about two of the most common mental illnesses:

Depression is a common medical illness that affects how you feel, think and act. Some symptoms include feeling sad, loss of interest in activities once enjoyed, feeling invaluable, and more. Everyone has different experiences with depression so try not to compare yourself to others!

Occasional feelings of anxiousness are common for most people, however, anxiety involves excessive fear and anxiousness. Anxiety disorders can cause people to avoid situations that trigger or worsen their symptoms. In order to be diagnosed with an anxiety disorder, the feeling of anxiety must be out of proportion of the situation and hinder your ability to function normally. Once again, this is normal! And, everyone’s experience with anxiety varies!

BlissBot

If you're feeling lonely, just talk to your new friend, BlissBot! All you have to do is type your answers to their questions. If you want to start a new conversation, just click the run/stop button on the top left. BlissBot is always here to talk to you!
